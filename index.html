1. Introduction

Topso'z is a fully offline dictionary application developed by Muhammad Mirkabilov. We respect and protect the privacy of our users.

2. Data Collection

Topso'z does not collect personal data. The app stores the following information locally on your device only:

  • Search history — your recent search queries
  • Favorites — words you have bookmarked
  • App settings — theme and font size preferences

This data is never sent to any server or shared with third parties.

3. Network Requests

The app uses internet permission only for displaying advertisements. Dictionary features work fully offline and do not require internet.

  • Google AdMob — to display advertisements

4. Advertising

The app uses Google AdMob. Google AdMob may collect device identifiers, IP addresses, and ad interaction data. See: Google Privacy Policy.

5. Children's Privacy

The app is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

6. User Rights

You can at any time:

  • Clear your search history
  • Remove your favorites list
  • Uninstall the app to delete all local data
  • Reset your Google advertising ID in device settings

This privacy policy may be updated from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page.

2. Сбор данных

Topso'z не собирает персональные данные. Приложение хранит следующую информацию только локально на вашем устройстве:

  • История поиска — ваши последние поисковые запросы

Приложение не предназначено для детей младше 13 лет. Мы сознательно не собираем личную информацию детей.
